自检：这段影像主要是合作项目，我操刀的剪辑，我觉得尝试了一下对假新闻、数字时代、实践的并行融合，因为巴比肯中心的真实历史与数字时代的基础设施设计再加上强行组合的泥塑实践实际在叙事上又平衡自洽了，这个算是我的小巧思……

Self Reflection: This footage primarily stems from a collaborative project, with editing handled by yours truly. I experimented with a parallel fusion of fake news, the digital age, and practical application. The Babican Centre's authentic historical context, coupled with its digital infrastructure design and the deliberately juxtaposed clay modelling practice, actually achieved a narrative balance and coherence. That was my little touch of ingenuity...
